How We Started

BritChoice Kenya was founded by business partners Peter and Maggie to solve a simple problem: the lack of access to genuine, high-quality household, skincare, and supplement brands from the UK. Having experienced the reliability of UK products firsthand, they realized many families in Kenya wanted these trusted brands but struggled with shipping delays, high rates, or counterfeit worries.

Together, Peter and Maggie personally select and import the inventory from authorized distributors and major retailers in the UK. By warehousing stock locally in Nairobi, they ensure every single item is 100% authentic and immediately ready for pick-up or delivery.

Returns & Refunds Policy

Simple and fair terms for exchanges and product returns.

At BritChoice Kenya, we take pride in importing 100% genuine UK products. Because we import household, hygiene, wellness, and personal care products directly from the United Kingdom, our policies are designed to protect quality and safety standards.

1. Inspection Upon Pick-up

We highly encourage all customers choosing **Self Pick-up** at Kahawa Sukari or CBD Phan Salon to inspect their items carefully at the point of collection to verify the seals, packaging, and quantity.

2. 48-Hour Return Window

We offer a **48-hour return window** after collection for unopened and fully sealed products in their original packaging under the following conditions:

  • The product has a manufacturer defect discovered after collection.
  • We accidentally fulfilled the incorrect brand, variant, or size.

3. Exclusions from Returns

Due to health, hygiene, and imported shelf-life standards, we **cannot** accept returns or issue refunds for:

  • Any product that has been opened, unsealed, or used.
  • Skincare or cosmetic items with broken seals.
  • Wellness supplements or vitamin packs that have been unsealed.
  • Products damaged due to customer misuse or improper storage after pick-up.

4. Refund Method

Approved returns can be **exchanged** for alternative products of equal value, or fully **refunded** via M-Pesa within 24 hours of item return verification.

Operational guidelines and customer agreements.

All prices listed on our website are in Kenyan Shillings (KES) and represent retail values. BritChoice Kenya acts as an independent reseller of imported British goods. We represent that all products are 100% genuine and sourced from established UK retailers. However, we are not the direct manufacturer of these items and are not liable for changes in product formulas or manufacturer defaults.

Privacy & Cookie Policy

How we respect and safeguard your personal data.

BritChoice Kenya is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. This policy outlines how we collect, store, and utilize your personal data.

1. What We Store Locally (LocalStorage)

Our website utilizes local browser storage (LocalStorage) instead of cookies to provide you with core shopping functions without tracking your web activity:

  • Shopping Cart (`britchoice_cart`): Stores your added items so they persist when you reload the page.
  • Guest Profile (`britchoice_profile`): Saves your Name, Phone, and Email locally so you don't have to re-type them on future checkouts.
  • Order History (`britchoice_orders`): Logs the past orders you placed via WhatsApp in this browser for your personal reference.

2. Zero Server Tracking Databases

We **do not** maintain central customer registration databases or passwords on our servers. Your contact details are stored entirely inside your own browser and are only shared with us when you click the WhatsApp link to submit an order.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, distribute, or lease your personal information to third parties. Your details are used strictly to coordinate order collection and delivery.
